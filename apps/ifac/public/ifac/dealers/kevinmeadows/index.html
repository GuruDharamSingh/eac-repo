Dealer: Kevin Meadows

Kevin Meadows is a versatile and experienced art dealer who specializes in acrylics, watercolors, pastels, charcoals and digital art.

He has been in the art business for over 35 years, starting as an artist himself and then expanding into selling and showcasing other artists’ work.

Kevin is always on the lookout for new opportunities to share his passion for art with others. He offers a range of services to his clients, such as appraisals, consultations, commissions and on-line sales.
