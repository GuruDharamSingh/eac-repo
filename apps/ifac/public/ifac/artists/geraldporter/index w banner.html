Artist: Gerald Porter

As an artist, as a painter, I suggest what you as an observer might notice as you unwittingly create your reality. I can help shape that reality to be more engaging and exciting. But since you as the observer are creating your own experience, I am ultimately serving as a mirror to reveal you to yourself. I am the master of ceremonies for a ritual in which you use my art to discover yourself. – Gerald Porter

Gerald Porter “was a professor, a university administrator, a painter, and a poet. He was a jack-of-all-trades who loved to unravel the mysteries of the universe. He always loved the weirdness in the world and had a knack for finding it. My father taught me to debate and to argue, he taught me how words could crack the surface of a problem or idea to find the layers of meaning and history that lay beneath. He always preferred the path less traveled.”
– David Bering- Porter, PhD.
