Artist: Dana McCool

As a surrealist, one contemplates the liminal threshold(s) of multidimensional reality.

Dana McCool is an interdisciplinary artist, designer, writer and anarchist. Using symbolism, color harmonies and universal archetypes, this artwork opens portals to the innermost, higher dimensions.

Current subject matter of interest includes ongoing experiential research in energy medicine modalities, meditation, parapsychology, trance mediumship, as well as several western and eastern esoteric traditions. These include, with emphasis, the legacy Work of G. I Gurdieff.

Dana is a graduate of OCAD University in Toronto, Canada. She has a background in religious studies, philosophy and sociology.
