Artist: E.J. Gold

California artist E.J. Gold is an internationally renowned artist, jazz musician and prolific writer.

Mr. Gold's art can be seen in museums, galleries and private collections.

E.J. Gold was the official artist for the International Association for Jazz Education (IAJE) from 2003 - 2008.
