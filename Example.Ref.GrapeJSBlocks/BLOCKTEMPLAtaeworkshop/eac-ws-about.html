About this workshop

This series approaches writing as a living practice — exploratory, embodied, and grounded in the present moment. Participants will move through prompts, response exercises, and group reflection across six weekly sessions.

No previous writing experience is necessary. What you bring is enough. The only requirement is a willingness to show up and pay attention.

Accessibility notes
This workshop takes place on the second floor. Unfortunately there is no elevator access at this location. Please get in touch if you have accessibility requirements and we will do our best to accommodate you.
