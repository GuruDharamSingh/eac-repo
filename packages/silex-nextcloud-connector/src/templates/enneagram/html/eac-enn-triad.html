Object Relations

Transactional / Superego

Driven by a sense of duty and responsibility, maintains a sanctified method for how things should be done in order to justify the right to meet its needs.

  • Body-Transactional (1): Considers it an ethical duty to maintain standards and enforce them.
  • Image-Transactional (2): Staying on each other’s good graces; keeping a positive self-image and sense of hope.
  • Mind-Transactional (6): Responsible to a cause, bond, or philosophy — the desire to do right by it and uphold it.
