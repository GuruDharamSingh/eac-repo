What is the Enneagram?

The Enneagram is a symbolic map of movement and transformation, rooted in esoteric mathematics, music theory through the law of octaves, the law of three, and the law of seven.

There are 9 points, each representing a general stage or quality which requires a specific change of quality to transform. Each point sits between two adjacent types — its wings — just as a musical scale moves from one tone to the next.

Consider it a radar scanner of identity that surfaces the core tensions and struggles you experience.
