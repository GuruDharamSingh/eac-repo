Artist Statement

My practice moves between material and memory — working across painting, drawing, and mixed media to explore the residue of place, body, and time. I am drawn to surfaces that hold evidence: the underpainting that shows through, the erased line that persists, the mark that refuses to disappear.

Much of my work begins with sustained observation of a single site — a room, a field, a body of water — and evolves through accumulated layers of response. The finished work is never a document of the place itself, but of the act of looking.

I work from a studio in Toronto and exhibit regularly across Canada and internationally. I am available for commissions, residencies, and collaborations.
