Practice & Medium

How I work

Primary medium
Oil on canvas / linen

Large and mid-scale paintings built through layered, slow-drying process. Palette knife and brush.

Secondary medium
Watercolour & drawing

Works on paper as study and as finished work. Responsive, field-based. Often precedes larger paintings.

Influences
Agnes Martin, Peter Doig, Tacita Dean

Drawn to work that is patient, material, and resistant to easy reading.

Process note

I begin with sustained time in a place — a field, a room, a body of water. Photographs, drawings, and notes accumulate over weeks before a canvas is stretched. The painting is rarely planned. It grows in response to what persists in memory and what resists reproduction.
